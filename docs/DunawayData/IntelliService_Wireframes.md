## B. Three-Page Brochure/Website Wireframe

This expands on the one-pager for users who want more detail.

---

### **Page 1: Homepage - The "Why"**
*   **Purpose:** Grab attention, state the core problem/solution, and drive visitors to learn more.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Hero Section:** Similar to the one-pager but more concise. Headline: `The End of Reactive Operations.` CTA: `[ Explore The Platform ]`.
    *   **Problem/Solution Summary:** A condensed version of the one-pager sections, visually showing "Before IntelliService" (chaos, silos) and "After IntelliService" (clarity, flow).
    *   **Feature Teaser:** Briefly introduce 3 core features with icons and a link to the Features page.
    *   **Testimonial:** One strong customer quote.
    *   **Footer.**

---

### **Page 2: Platform & Features - The "How"**
*   **Purpose:** Detail the technology and capabilities for a more technical or thorough audience.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Hero Section:** `The Most Powerful & Flexible Operations Platform.`
    *   **Platform Architecture Diagram:** A clean diagram showing:
        *   `Left Side:` Data Sources (ERP, CRM, IoT Sensors, Databases).
        *   `Center:` IntelliService Platform (Integration Fabric, Cognitive Core, Workflow Engine, Unified Database).
        *   `Right Side:` Outcomes (Unified Dashboard, Predictive Alerts, Automated Actions).
    *   **Detailed Feature Breakdowns:** Each feature gets a full section with a small screenshot/diagram and detailed text.
        *   `Feature 1: The Cognitive Core` (explains the reasoning engine).
        *   `Feature 2: The Predictive Engine` (explains the health monitoring & forecasting).
        *   `Feature 3: The Integration Fabric` (explains the pre-built connectors and API).
        *   `Feature 4: The Workflow Automator` (explains the rule-based automation).
    *   **Technology Section:** Logos of key technologies (Python, PyTorch, Kubernetes, PostgreSQL, etc.) to build trust with technical evaluators.
    *   **CTA Section:** `[ See It In Action - Request a Demo ]`
    *   **Footer.**

---

### **Page 3: Solutions & Pricing - The "What For"**
*   **Purpose:** Show how IntelliService applies to specific customer needs and outline how to buy.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Hero Section:** `A Solution for Every Team.`
    *   **Solutions by Industry:** Tabs or sections for key verticals.
        *   `Manufacturing:` "Predict equipment failure, optimize production lines."
        *   `Logistics:` "Forecast supply chain disruptions, automate shipment tracking."
        *   `Retail:` "Optimize inventory, analyze in-store customer flow."
    *   **Solutions by Role:** Sections for key personas.
        *   `For the COO:` "Gain complete operational visibility."
        *   `For the Plant Manager:` "Go from reactive to predictive maintenance."
        *   `For the IT Director:` "Securely unify legacy and modern systems."
    *   **Pricing Tiers:** A clear 3-tier pricing table.
        *   `Tier 1: Professional` (Core features, limited users/workflows).
        *   `Tier 2: Business` (All features, more users/workflows, priority support).
        *   `Tier 3: Enterprise` (Unlimited everything, dedicated support, custom integrations).
    *   **Contact Form:** A detailed form for requesting a demo or a quote.
    *   **Footer.**

## C. 10-Slide Pitch Deck Wireframe

This is a classic seed-stage pitch deck structure.

---

### **Slide 1: Title**
*   `[IntelliService Logo]`
*   **Title:** `IntelliService`
*   **Tagline:** `The Autonomous Operating System for Enterprise`

---

### **Slide 2: The Problem**
*   **Headline:** `Enterprises are losing billions to operational friction.`
*   **Visual:** Three simple, stark stats.
    *   `Stat 1:` `40%` of unplanned downtime is preventable.
    *   `Stat 2:` Knowledge workers spend `25%` of their time on manual, repetitive tasks.
    *   `Stat 3:` `85%` of companies struggle to use their data effectively.
*   **Summary:** `Data is siloed, teams are reactive, and potential is wasted.`

---

### **Slide 3: The Solution**
*   **Headline:** `We turn operational data into autonomous action.`
*   **Visual:** A simple 3-part graphic: `[Data In]` -> `[IntelliService Brain]` -> `[Automated Outcome]`.
*   **One-liner:** `IntelliService is a SaaS platform that connects to all your business systems and uses AI to predict issues and automate workflows *before* they become problems.`

---

### **Slide 4: How It Works**
*   **Headline:** `Connect, Reason, and Automate`
*   **Visual:** Three columns with icons.
    *   **Connect:** Our "Integration Fabric" unifies data from any source (ERP, CRM, IoT).
    *   **Reason:** Our "Cognitive Core" analyzes real-time data to identify patterns and predict future events.
    *   **Automate:** Our "Workflow Engine" executes complex, multi-step actions based on configurable rules.

---

### **Slide 5: The "Magic" - Key Differentiators**
*   **Headline:** `Why We Win`
*   **Point 1: Predictive, Not Just Reactive.** `We predict and prevent downtime, we don't just report on it after the fact.`
*   **Point 2: Composable & Flexible.** `Unlike monolithic ERPs, our fabric approach integrates with the tools you already use.`
*   **Point 3: Explainable AI.** `Our reasoning engine shows you *why* an alert was triggered, building trust and enabling faster human response.`

---

### **Slide 6: Market Opportunity**
*   **Headline:** `A $50B Market Ripe for Disruption`
*   **Visual:** A large circle representing the Total Addressable Market (TAM) with smaller circles for Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM).
*   **TAM:** Global Market for Operational Intelligence & Automation - `$50B`.
*   **SAM:** Mid-to-Large Enterprises in Manufacturing & Logistics - `$15B`.
*   **SOM:** Initial target of 500 companies in North America - `$500M`.

---

### **Slide 7: Competitive Landscape**
*   **Headline:** `A New Category of Platform`
*   **Visual:** A 2x2 matrix.
    *   `Y-Axis:` Proactive & Predictive (High) vs. Reactive & Historical (Low).
    *   `X-Axis:` Composable & Flexible (High) vs. Monolithic & Rigid (Low).
*   **Placement:**
    *   `Bottom-Left:` Legacy ERPs (Oracle, SAP).
    *   `Bottom-Right:` Modern BI Tools (Tableau, Power BI).
    *   `Top-Left:` Point AI Solutions (niche predictive maintenance tools).
    *   `Top-Right:` **`[IntelliService Logo]`** (alone in this quadrant).

---

### **Slide 8: The Team**
*   **Headline:** `The Right Team to Build It`
*   **Visual:** Professional headshots and titles for 2-4 key founders.
*   **Founder 1:** `Name - CEO` (e.g., "Ex-Director of Ops at [Major Mfg. Co.], scaled teams from 10 to 200.")
*   **Founder 2:** `Name - CTO` (e.g., "Lead AI Engineer at [Big Tech Co.], published research in predictive modeling.")
*   **Advisors (optional):** Logos or names of key industry advisors.

---

### **Slide 9: Traction & Financials**

*   **Traction (if any):** "`5` pilot customers signed", "`$50k` in early revenue", "`150%` MoM growth in waitlist signups".
